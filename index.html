How to Use

  • Click "Start Scanner" to activate your camera
  • Point your camera at a QR code
  • The scanner will automatically stop after detecting a QR code
  • Scan results will appear in the results box
  • Use "Scan Again" to scan another QR code

Note: Requires a browser that supports the BarcodeDetector API (Chrome/Edge/Android browsers)
