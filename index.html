How to Use

  • Click "Start Scanner" to slide down the QR scanner
  • Point your camera at a QR code - scanner will detect automatically
  • The scanner will slide away after successful detection
  • Scan results will appear right below the scanner area
  • Works on Firefox Mobile, Chrome, and other browsers
